## Summary

Using panel data, the authors estimate that a [[Bequeathment]] motive is significantly larger than predicted by [[Mortality Risk and Bequests]]. Authors suggest bequeatment motive is not shared equally. Not affected by prescence of children. However, they cannot distinguish between a bequeathment motive and another motive consistent with [[Precautionary Saving]].
